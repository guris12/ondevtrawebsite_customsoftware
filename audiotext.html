Introduction

The new Transcribe App (iOS 26) is designed to make note-taking effortless. Speak naturally, and AI turns your words into accurate, punctuated text, automatically organized into clean paragraphs. Whether you're in a lecture hall, a boardroom, or a noisy café, it adapts to your context and ensures nothing is lost.

Key Features

  • 🎤 Accurate speech-to-text with punctuation
  • 📝 Structured paragraphs & auto-summaries
  • 📌 Insert highlights and edit inline
  • 🌐 Multi-language support + translation
  • 📶 Works offline — no Wi-Fi required
  • 📤 Export to email, PDF, or note apps

Customization

Personalize your transcription experience:

  • Choose larger fonts for readability
  • Switch to dark mode for eye comfort
  • Flip text to face your audience in real time
  • Set language preferences and custom hotkeys

Use Cases

The Transcribe App fits seamlessly into everyday workflows:

  • Students — take lecture notes without missing details.
  • Professionals — record and summarize meetings.
  • Journalists — transcribe interviews quickly.
  • Accessibility — provide captions for hearing-impaired users.
  • Content creators — generate blog drafts and scripts directly from voice notes.
  • Medical & Legal fields — create structured notes from complex conversations.

Privacy & Security

All processing is secure, private, and ad-free. Offline mode ensures your data stays on-device. There are no hidden uploads — your words belong entirely to you.

FAQ

Does it work offline? Yes, transcription runs fully on-device.

Can I export transcripts? Absolutely, as PDFs, Word docs, or direct email notes.

Is translation included? Yes, dozens of languages with optional inline translation.
