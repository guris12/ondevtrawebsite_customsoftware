Automated Multi-Currency Invoicing

A freelancer needed to automate dual-currency invoicing across Stripe India (INR) and Apple Inc (USD). We built a Flask web app that reads bank PDFs, extracts amounts, and generates compliant invoices with sequential numbering.

  • Reads bank PDF statements, extracts amounts automatically
  • Generates invoices with shared sequential numbering
  • INR ↔ USD exchange rates from remittance PDFs
